WCWAA 2026 Spring — Hitter Archetype & Pitching Approach Reference
==================================================================
Generated from gen_reports.py  |  All thresholds are code-exact.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PART 1 — STAT DEFINITIONS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Stat    Full Name                  Formula
------  -------------------------  -----------------------------------------
AVG     Batting Average            H ÷ AB
OBP     On-Base Percentage         (H + BB + HBP) ÷ (AB + BB + HBP)
SLG     Slugging Percentage        Total Bases ÷ AB
C%      Contact Rate               (AB − K) ÷ AB   (BB/HBP excluded from AB)
GB%     Ground Ball Rate           Ground ball BIP ÷ total BIP
FB+LD%  Fly Ball + Line Drive Rate (Fly ball + line drive BIP) ÷ total BIP
SM%     Swing-and-Miss Rate        Swing-and-miss ÷ total swings
                                   (swings = swing-miss + fouls + in-play)
CStr%   Called Strike Rate         Called strikes ÷ total pitches seen
FPT%    First Pitch Take Rate      First-pitch takes ÷ (takes + swings)
                                   Only counts PAs where pitch data exists.
                                   Higher = more patient / takes more pitches.

Note: SM% and FPT% require pitch sequence data in the game file.
      If unavailable, the stat shows "--" on the card.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PART 2 — ARCHETYPE COMPONENTS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The archetype is a two-word label:  [APPROACH]  [RESULT]

─── APPROACH (based on plate discipline / first-pitch behavior) ────

Approach      Trigger condition                        What it means
-----------   ---------------------------------------  ---------------------------------
Aggressive    SM% ≥ 55% AND SM% > CStr% × 1.5         Swinging aggressively and missing
              (overrides FPT-based rules)
Aggressive    FPT% < 40%                               Hacks at first pitch > 60% of time
Disciplined   FPT% 40–70% AND OBP − AVG ≥ .060        Takes pitches selectively; walks
Passive       FPT% > 70%                               Takes too many — often frozen on K
Aggressive    FPT% < 55% (fallback when gap too small) Default swing-first bucket
Passive       FPT% ≥ 55% (fallback)                    Default take-first bucket

If no FPT data is available: defaults to "Disciplined."

─── RESULT (based on production / contact quality) ─────────────────

Result        Trigger condition                        What it means
-----------   ---------------------------------------  ---------------------------------
Walker        BB/PA in top-33% of roster               On base via walks, not hits
              AND BB/(H+BB) > 33%
              AND C% < 70%
              AND OBP − AVG ≥ .100
Overmatched   SM% > 50%  (hard)                        Can't make contact
              OR  C% ≤ bottom-33% of roster  (hard)
              OR  SM% ≥ 35% AND C% < 65% AND AVG < .250  (soft combined)
Power         SLG in top-33% of roster                 Extra-base hit threat
              AND (SLG ≥ 1.2 × AVG  OR  ISO ≥ .120)
Contact       Everything else (default)                Puts ball in play consistently

Evaluation order: Walker → Overmatched → Power → Contact
(Walker is checked first; if a player qualifies for both Overmatched
 and Power, Overmatched wins.)

─── PA GATE ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

PA count      Label shown
-----------   --------------------------
< 5 PA        "—"  (no label assigned)
5–9 PA        Label + "*"  (e.g. "Aggressive Contact*")
≥ 10 PA       Clean label  (e.g. "Aggressive Contact")

PART 4 — PITCHING APPROACH DESCRIPTIONS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Approach          Used for                     Intent
----------------  ---------------------------  -----------------------------------------
Edges + Mix Speed Aggressive Power/Contact      Hitter is attacking — keep the ball on edges
                                               and change speeds to disrupt timing
Climb the Ladder  Aggressive Overmatched        Already swinging and missing; elevate
                                               fastballs to chase upward in the zone
Outside - In      Aggressive Walker             Patient hitter who also hacks first pitch;
                                               start off-speed away, work back in to steal
                                               strikes without giving a pitch to pull
Keep Mixing       Disciplined Power/Contact     Disciplined hitter with production; no single
                                               pitch gets them out — vary location and speed
Attack the Zone   Disciplined/Passive Walker,   Gets on base via walks or freezes on strikes;
                  Passive/Disciplined Overm.    throw strikes early to force weak contact
Attack & Expand   Passive Power/Contact         Takes first pitch — start in the zone to force
                                               a swing decision, then expand to chase pitches
